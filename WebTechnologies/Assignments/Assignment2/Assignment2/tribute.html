Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam

Dr. Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. He was known as the "Missile Man of India" and a beloved educator.

Achievements

  • Led development of Agni and Prithvi missiles
  • Played key role in Pokhran-II nuclear tests
  • Served as President from 2002–2007
  • Wrote inspirational books like *Wings of Fire*
  • Champion of youth, innovation, and education
